Noun/Rzeczownik: A word that names a person, place, thing, or idea.
Pronoun/Zaimek: A word that replaces a noun.
Verb/Czasownik: A word that expresses action or a state of being.
Adjective/Przymiotnik: A word that describes or modifies a noun or pronoun.
Adverb/Przysłówek: A word that modifies a verb, adjective, or another adverb.
Preposition/Przyimek: A word that shows the relationship between a noun or pronoun and other words in the sentence.
Conjunction/Spójnik: A word that connects words, phrases, or clauses.
Interjection/Wykrzyknik: A word that expresses strong emotion.
